The RISC-V instruction set architecture is rapidly transforming artificial intelligence development. As an open and extensible architecture, RISC-V provides unique opportunities for hardware customization, enabling developers to create specialized operating systems and neural network libraries that precisely meet the computational needs of emerging embedded devices. Whether enhancing energy efficiency or enabling custom instructions for specific cognitive functions, this architecture plays a pivotal role in building scalable systems. By allowing engineers to design highly customized hardware interfaces, it provides an ideal foundation for modeling complex machine learning processes and developing advanced systems inspired by biological brain mechanisms from the ground up.
